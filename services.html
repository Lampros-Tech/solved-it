K12 Textbook Solutions -
Video and Text

When it comes to educational content development, K12 is the hottest category and we at Solved It have put our efforts where it matters the most. With a talented line of SMEs, trained and monitored by the experts of the industry, we are equipped in executing this task of creating K12 textbook solutions with maximum volume and minimum reworks.

We at Solved It are equipped to create K12 textbook solutions starting from standard 6 to standard 12. This includes content covering all the boards, i.e. NCERT, ICSE/ISC, IGCSE and all the state boards across the country. And that’s not all, we can deliver those textbook solutions in local, vernacular languages as well, along with the regular languages like English and Hindi. This ensures that now anybody and everybody can reap in the benefits of these textbook solutions, irrespective of their medium of instruction and of course, geographical location.

Apart from the regular K12 textbook solutions we also accept projects for creating solutions of popular reference materials – R.D. Sharma in mathematics, H.C. Verma in physics and Selina for ISCE to name a few. We also deliver text and video solutions of previous year papers of 10th and 12th along with competitive examinations like JEE (Mains/Advance) and NEET.

Text and Videos solutions
for popular competitive examinations.

Alongside the K12 textbook solutions, we at Solved It are also in line to produce solutions pertaining to popular competitive examinations like UPSC, Bank SSC, Bank PO, NDA, Regional Civil Services examinations, etc. In this category we try to provide the solutions as requi your business which could include previous year papers, popular reference materials, specimen papers, etc.

Now it is evident that K12 textbook solutions is the hottest category when we talk about educational content development, but this arena of competitive examinations is often overlooked by the businesses or understandably, are not on the top of their priority list. But there seems to be a monumental scope in this category as availability of solutions for these examinations is quite scarce and also the competition is relatively narrow. Thus it’s a win-win, both, from business point of view as well as the consumer point of view.
